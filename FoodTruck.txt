<section id="description">
  <h2>I. General Company Description</h2>
  <p><strong>Business Opportunity:</strong> Provide Bridgewater College students with quality food when traditional dining hours don’t fit their schedules.</p>
  <p><strong>Problem Solved:</strong> Hunger, high food costs, and limited options.</p>
  <p><strong>Solution:</strong> More accessible, affordable, and accommodating food through a mobile food truck.</p>
  <p><strong>Business Type:</strong> Service (Mobile Food Truck)</p>
  <p><strong>Ownership:</strong> LLC – to reduce liability and ensure operational flexibility.</p>
  <p><strong>Mission Statement:</strong> We provide what college students need because we've been through it. We accommodate and satisfy every customer.</p>
  <p><strong>Social Responsibility:</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li>Donate unsold food to shelters</li>
    <li>Partner with student organizations</li>
    <li>Hire students and offer flexible work hours</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section id="products">
  <h2>II. Products and Services</h2>
  <p><strong>Menu Offerings:</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li>Burgers & Sandwiches (vegetarian options too)</li>
    <li>Tacos & Wraps</li>
    <li>Fries, onion rings, mozzarella sticks</li>
    <li>Soft drinks, coffee, desserts</li>
  </ul>
  <p><strong>Competitive Advantages:</strong> Late hours, fresh quality, cooked-to-order food</p>
  <p><strong>Pricing:</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li>Entrées: $6 - $10</li>
    <li>Sides: $2 - $4</li>
    <li>Drinks: $1 - $3</li>
    <li>Combos: $8 - $12</li>
  </ul>
</section>
